# AI Feature Proposal

## IRCTC Travel Assistant

### Problem

Users frequently struggle with:

* Train selection
* Waiting list understanding
* PNR interpretation
* TDR filing

### AI Solution

An AI-powered assistant integrated into IRCTC.

### User Examples

User:
"Will my WL 23 ticket get confirmed...?"

AI:
"Based on historical trends, confirmation chances are approximately medium. Check again closer to departure."

---

User:
"What does RAC mean?"

AI:
"RAC means Reservation Against Cancellation. You can board the train but may share a berth."

---

User:
"Which train should I choose?"

AI:
"Train A has higher seat availability and shorter travel duration."

### Benefits

* Faster decision making
* Reduced customer support load
* Better first-time user experience
